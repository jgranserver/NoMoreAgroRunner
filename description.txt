NoMoreAgroRunner Mod

Are you tired of chaotic boss battles? NoMoreAgroRunner Mod is here to enhance your multiplayer experience by improving coordination during boss fights.

Features:

Multiplayer Focus: Designed to help players work together more effectively.
Warning System: Alerts players when they are targeted by bosses and going too far from the other players.
Easy Integration: Works seamlessly with tModLoader with no complicated setup.

Take your boss battles to the next level with NoMoreAgroRunner Mod. Perfect for those who enjoy multiplayer gameplay and want a smoother, more coordinated experience.
